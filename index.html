The Problem

In the university environment, students frequently forget or lose personal items. Current processes for finding and recovering lost items are inefficient and time-consuming. Existing solutions include posting on social media groups (e.g., HCMUT K22, K23...) or handing items to security guards, but these face issues like information overload, fragmentation, and lack of centralized tracking.

Why Choose Us?

Designed specifically for the HCMUT community

Target Users

The platform is designed for students and staff at HCMUT Campus 2 (Di An), verified via @hcmut.edu.vn email. It focuses on the university community to ensure relevance and security.

Value Proposition

Lost & Found HCMUT provides a specialized, structured, and anonymous way to post, search, and recover lost items. It minimizes hassle by offering location-based postings, real-time notifications, and scam prevention.

Key Features

Everything you need to recover your items safely and quickly.

Structured Postings

Post lost or found items with images, categorized by specific buildings/areas (e.g., H1, H2) to eliminate information clutter.

Anonymity & Security

Interact anonymously without revealing personal contact info. Internal messaging avoids public exposure.

Smart Notifications

Subscribe to specific areas for instant notifications on new postings relevant to you.

Verification Workflow

Claimers provide detailed descriptions to verify ownership, preventing scams and ensuring items return to owners.

Data Privacy

Collect anonymous data for potential school statistics while strictly ensuring user privacy.

Modern Tech Stack

Built with React Native/Expo for mobile, FastAPI for backend, and deployed on Google Cloud.
